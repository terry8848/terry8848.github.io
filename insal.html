关于游戏

Time only moves when you move. Look around, plan your actions, and only by starting to move, you start time again. Smash enemies to pieces with a pistol shot. Use a machine gun to fire heavy fire. Destroy multiple enemies with one shotgun blast. Take weapons straight from the hands of the enemy. Explode gas cylinders and throw objects and weapons at enemies. Dodge bullets in slow motion. Features: - unique gameplay - a variety of weapons - minimalistic graphics - interactive items - realistic physics of damage to enemies

如何玩

Left Mouse Button - pick up / shoot weapon Right Mouse Button - throw weapon WASD - movement Speed up time by moving.
